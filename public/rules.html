Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock (RPSLS)

  • Paper COVERS Rock

  • Spock SMASHES Scissors

  • Lizard EATS Paper
  • Paper DISPROVES Spock
  • Spock VAPORIZES Rock
